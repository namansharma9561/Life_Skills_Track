# Grit and Growth Mindset


## 1. Grit

Grit means not giving up on your goals, even when things get hard. It is about staying strong, patient, and consistent.


## 2. Growth Mindset

A growth mindset is the belief that you can improve with practice and effort. It means you can get better at anything if you keep learning.


## 3. Internal Locus of Control

**Internal Locus of Control:** Believing that your actions decide your success, not luck or others.

**Key Point:** You stay motivated when you take responsibility for your life and focus on what you can control.


## 4. How to Build a Growth Mindset

- Accept challenges
- Learn from mistakes
- Focus on effort
- Practice regularly
- Stay curious
- Ask for feedback
- Keep improving


## 5. How will you build a Growth Mindset?

- Take on new challenges.
- Learn from failures instead of feeling bad.
- Practice difficult things daily.
- Keep a positive attitude.
- Ask for help or feedback to improve.
